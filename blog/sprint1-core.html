My Cultural Identity And Values

I was brought up in a religious family. My family and I go to church every Sunday. We believe in the Bible and we are taught in church to have a close relationship with God. Here's the summary of my personal values: God, husband, extended family/children, work/ministry and friends.

I admit that there are several times wherein my personal values where put to test. There were stages in my life where I made bad decisions because of my stubborness and there were times wherein I've lost my way. But I have to say, I always seem to gravitate towards God and then I would remember my values in life.

I love people. I notice over the years, my family and friends come to me for advice when they are facing crossroads in their lives. They also come to me because I am a good listener. I truly believe God has given me this gift of a "listening ear". I listen to people through my body language and facial expression. Having said this, I am also introvert by nature. I don't mind spending time with people but I have to make sure I recharge my energy alone. Spending time just by myself is crucial in my everyday living.
